Probability - Key Formulas

Basic Probability

Probability of an event = Number of favorable outcomes / Total number of possible outcomes

P(A) = n(A) / n(S)

Complement Rule

Probability of event not occurring = 1 - Probability of event occurring

P(A') = 1 - P(A)

Addition Rule

Union of Events: P(A or B) = P(A) + P(B) - P(A and B)

Mutually Exclusive Events: P(A or B) = P(A) + P(B)

Multiplication Rule

Independent Events: P(A and B) = P(A) × P(B)

Dependent Events: P(A and B) = P(A) × P(B|A)
